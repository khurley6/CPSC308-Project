Hello SMC Math and Computer Science students! Welcome to your centralized portal — a space designed just for you. This page brings together important links and information that are often useful but not always easy to track down. Whether you're looking for program requirements, study abroad info, or just a reminder of what goes into your portfolio, it's all right here — in one convenient place.

Here’s a quick guide to what you’ll find in each section:

  • Home: A landing page and warm welcome to the portal.
  • Programs: Details on our majors and minors, including course requirements and faculty bios.
  • Why Math: Explore the benefits of studying math, career paths, and internship opportunities.
  • Student Resources: Find info on study abroad as a math major, SMC math-related clubs, year-by-year portfolio expectations, and how to prepare for your senior comprehensive project.
