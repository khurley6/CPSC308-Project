Why Study Mathematics?

Benefits

Majoring in Mathematics provides a wide array of benefits for students as they approach post-grad life.

Enhanced Critical Thinking and Problem-Solving Skills

Mathematics provides ways to approach complex problems, such as identifying unknowns, formulating a plan, and evaluating solutions.

Transferable Skills

The skills learned in math, such as data analysis, organization, and communication, are valuable in many different fields and aspects of life.

Real-World Applications

Math is an essential part of everyday life. It is included in logical reasoning, and everyday tasks such as filing taxes, managing funds, and understanding statistical concepts.

Diverse Career Paths

A strong math foundation can open doors to numerous careers, including those in STEM fields, finance, law, business, and education.

Job Opportunities

There are countless career paths available for anyone with a degree in Mathematics because of the analytical and problem-solving skills students develop along the way.

Finance and Investment: Financial analyst, investment analyst, financial planner, actuary, auditor, accountant

Technology: Software engineer, data scientist, data analyst, computer programmer, computer hardware developer, video game designer

Statistics and Research: Statistician, market research analyst, economist, operations research analyst, research scientist

Education: Mathematics teacher, college professor

Internship Opportunities

A math degree opens doors to a wide variety of internship opportunities, especially in areas like data analysis, research, and even finance. Math majors often seek internships from a place of career exploration, wanting to develop their skills, networking, and career advancement.

Common Internship Areas

Finance: Financial analyst, investment banking intern, accounting intern

Data Analysis: Data science intern, business intelligence intern

Research: Quantitative research analyst intern, research analyst, mathematics researcher

Technology: Software development intern, cybersecurity intern, project management intern

Actuarial Science: Actuarial intern
